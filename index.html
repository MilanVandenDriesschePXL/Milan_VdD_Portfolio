About Me
Designing is my passion

Hi, my name is Milan, a 20-year-old student from Belgium. I'm currently studying at PXL-Digital, where I’m honing my skills in Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma. My journey into design is anything but conventional. I initially pursued a career as a professional football player, and while life took me in a different direction, my background in sports taught me invaluable lessons. Discipline, teamwork, and a relentless work ethic are qualities I carry with me into every project I take on. When I’m not immersed in design or academics, I enjoy spending my free time gaming and hitting the gym. These activities keep me energized, focused, and ready to tackle new challenges. I’m excited about the future and eager to work on innovative projects that allow me to grow both creatively and professionally.
